Sandvich Recipe

A grilled cheese sandwich is a classic comfort food made with toasted bread and melted cheese. It is crispy on the outside, warm and gooey on the inside, making it a popular quick snack or light meal. The sandwich is cooked in a pan until golden brown, allowing the cheese to melt evenly. Its simplicity and rich taste make it a timeless and easy-to-make recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Cheese slices
  • Butter

Here are the basic steps for making lasagna:

  1. Butter one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place cheese between the slices (butter side outside).
  3. Heat a pan and cook the sandwich on medium heat.
  4. Fry until both sides are golden brown and the cheese melts.
  5. Cut and serve.
