Omelette Recipe

An omelette is a quick and simple dish made from eggs, milk, and basic seasonings. It is light, nutritious, and perfect for breakfast or a fast meal during the day. The eggs are whisked and cooked in a pan until soft and fluffy, creating a smooth texture and mild flavor. It can also be easily customized with cheese, vegetables, or herbs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Butter or oil

Here are the basic steps for making lasagna:

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk with milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat a pan and add butter or oil.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the pan.
  4. Cook on medium heat until the omelette sets.
  5. Fold and serve warm.
